Patrick Barks

coding, statistics, ecology

I’m currently a post-doctoral fellow at the University of Southern Denmark where I study plant demography, teach data analysis to Master’s students, and contribute to R packages that help others do interesting analyses.

R package contributions

  • Rcompadre - access to the COM(P)ADRE demographic databases
  • Rage - tools for demographic analysis
  • rBBS - access to the North American Breeding Bird Survey
  • cantempr - access to Canadian homogenized temperature data

Web apps

  • Canada-Clim - a D3.js app to examine Canadian climate trends
  • Shiny-Bayes - a Shiny app to illustrate Bayesian inference
